To Infnity and Beyond #2



The second in the "To Infinity and Beyond" posters, this one, still keeping to the theme of space, finds our tiny rocket back on Earth preapring for lift off. For this poster I really wanted to play around with typography and somehow make it feel more dynamic. I experimented quite a bit but lastly I settled with playing with the layering, with some words placed behind and some infront of the rocket.
