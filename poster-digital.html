To Infnity and Beyond #1



The first in a series of two. When tasked with creating 2 posters using the tagline "To Infinity and Beyond", I wanted the 2 posters to be strikingly different. This one was based around the theme of space and immediately I knew what I wanted it to look like. A large green planet, backed with a purple void with a tiny spaceship orbiting it, leaving the tagline to be formed in the smoke trail.
