Sludge



This was produced for a fictional brief I was handed as a task for my course. The brief was for a vegan energy drink company called 'Sludge' and we had to create branding for it. My can includes a large, green slime monster named Bernie, with the brand's name placed in his mouth. He came to me out of nowhere and formed into this design I love through a lot of sketching and development. I wanted a design which was edgy and fun and I mean what edgy teen doesn't like monsters?
