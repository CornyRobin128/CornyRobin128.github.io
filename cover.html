Power Shield Cover



This is the cover of a book we were tasked in producing for one of my units. My book detailed the jounrey of a young comic fan who woke up one morning and found himself in the comic book world of his own creation. The cover design was heavily inspired by Marvel's 'Ultimate Graphic Novel Collection'. A mature style of a black glossy background with large focus on the emblem in the middle breaking out of the confines of the box it's based in.
