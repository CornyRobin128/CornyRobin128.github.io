About Me



Hi, my name is Connor, I am a student who is currently enrolled in a B.A of Graphic Design. Prior to this I have graduated in an Advanced Diploma in Creative Media Production. I love branching out and expanding my capabilities in order to be the best version of my self I can be. I love delving into the arts, whether it be through drawing or through a good book, but I am not afraid to take it easy and watch some TV or spend time with my friends. I am ambitious and hardworking and always promise myself to do my very best at any task thrown my way.
